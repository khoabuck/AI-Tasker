# <a name="_nobz5x6qlx1y"></a>**📘 AI Marketplace Platform for AI Automation Services (AITasker)**
## <a name="_6ltkt4zeuw8j"></a>**1. Topic**
**AI Marketplace Platform for AI Automation Services (AITasker)**
## <a name="_m0cvv4kf5xyz"></a>**2. Context**
In recent years, AI adoption has grown rapidly across businesses, but small and non-technical companies still face many barriers:

- Lack of technical expertise to implement AI solutions
- Difficulty in defining project requirements
- No specialized platform for AI service outsourcing

Meanwhile:

- AI experts lack visibility and a trusted channel to monetize their skills
- Existing freelance platforms are too generic and not optimized for AI services

👉 AITasker aims to bridge this gap by providing a specialized AI marketplace with integrated AI assistance tools — improving matching efficiency, reducing project ambiguity, and increasing the success rate of AI projects.
## <a name="_lxb2zmpx0trd"></a>**3. Problems**
Current problems include:

- No AI-specialized marketplace → hard to find qualified experts
- Poor requirement definition from clients → high project failure risk
- Lack of trust in payment, delivery quality, and dispute handling
- No intelligent recommendation system for expert matching
- Fragmented workflow: hiring, communication, and payment are not integrated

**AITasker solves these problems through:**

- AI-assisted requirement generation
- Expert recommendation system
- Escrow-based secure payment
- End-to-end project lifecycle management
## <a name="_3oo53s7m7luv"></a>**4. Primary Actors**
### <a name="_bke5thc2w6ww"></a>**Clients**
(Businesses / Startups / Non-technical founders)

- Need AI solutions for business automation
- Post jobs and hire AI experts
- Manage projects and payments
### <a name="_aqzypdfju8n5"></a>**AI Experts**
(Freelancers / Engineers / Consultants)

- Provide AI-related services
- Submit proposals and manage deliverables
- Earn income through the platform
### <a name="_jijsjapi0nu4"></a>**Admin**
- Manage platform operations
- Handle disputes and moderation
- Monitor analytics and transactions
## <a name="_q4m2dhcffyir"></a>**5. Functional Requirements**
### <a name="_xo5xf9k3pled"></a>**5.1 Client Side**
- Create job postings (title, description, budget, timeline, required skills)
- Browse AI services marketplace
- Hire experts and manage projects
- Track milestones and approve deliverables
- Real-time messaging/chat system
- Secure payment through escrow
- View transaction history and reviews
### <a name="_6ly3usgu6ehq"></a>**5.2 Expert Side**
- Create profile and portfolio
- Publish AI services
- Submit proposals for jobs
- Manage projects and deliverables
- Track earnings and withdraw money
### <a name="_66lp48phnj0l"></a>**5.3 AI Modules**
- **AI Job Assistant**: suggest better job descriptions, budgets, required skills
- **AI Service Generator**: auto-generate service descriptions
- **AI Expert Recommendation**: match suitable experts with client jobs
### <a name="_i9ptpi6zg5q6"></a>**5.4 Admin Functions**
- Manage users, jobs, and services
- Handle disputes
- Monitor transactions and analytics
## <a name="_2qlwxjqop67t"></a>**6. Research Questions**
- **RQ1**: How to improve matching accuracy between AI experts and client requirements?
- **RQ2**: How effective are AI-assisted tools in helping non-technical users define project scope?
- **RQ3**: What factors influence trust and successful transactions in AI service marketplaces?
